DÉcor and Furniture

Create the look you envision with fabric backdrops.

Backdrops can be used to create a focal point behind a head table, ceiling, outdoor patio, or transform a blank space. By using drapery tiebacks and fabric swags, you can provide an added touch to your space.

You can also create a fresh look and feel to your event with canopies, cabanas, columns, and chuppahs. Whether you want your DJ to be enclosed in a cabana, have a lounge area for your guests, or want to enhance the overall look of your venue, Concept Effects can help.

By combining your fabric design with lighting, you can enhance the overall atmosphere of the room and completely transform the space.

Dance under the stars with a white dance floor or LED Dance floor.

Turn your lounge area in to a VIP atmosphere with white leather lounge furniture and our custom LED furniture. Pricing available upon request for our exclusive line of LED and Lounge Furniture

Photo Booths

We are pleased to offer mobile photo booths! Whether you are looking to have a photo booth at your wedding, Corporate Party, Graduation Party, Bridal Shower, or bar/bat mitzvahs, Photo Booths give your guests, on the night/day of the event, creative freedom when taking a picture and leave them with something to take home.

All Photo booths are wheelchair accessible and come with an attendant. Each package can be customized and tailored to what the client is looking for.

Afterwards, our clients are given a password-protected gallery where they will find all of the photos that were taken the night of their event.

A picture captures a thousand words. A photo booth captures endless memories!!!

Video Booths and Green Screens available upon request.

Photography/Videography

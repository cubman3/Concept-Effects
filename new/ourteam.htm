About Us

Laura Olsen
Interior Design and Consulting.

LauraOlsen@concepteffects.com

After graduating from the Harrington Institute of Interior Design, Laura has developed a successful interior design career over the past 15 years. She is an Allied Member of the American Society of Interior Designers (ASID). Her projects have ranged from small, individual rooms to comprehensive home designs--her intuitive ability to understand a client's specific design style and daily-living needs helps people enjoy their homes to the fullest. Laura believes that interior design is a marriage of art and lifestyle, as well as a service she looks forward to providing for her many long-term clients.

Angela Pesa
Make-up, Marketing, and Planning.

AngelaPesa@concepteffects.com

Founder of Angela Makeup Chicago, Angela is a Chicago based makeup artist that specializes in airbrush, bridal and special FX makeup. Born and raised in the Chicago land area Angela graduated from DePaul University with a concentration in communications, marketing, and theatre makeup. Angela uses her combined educational background to create the most unique message, idea, and look that a client could only imagine possible. As a business owner herself, Angela understands the type of foundation that needs to be laid in order to create a successful event that is memorable to all of the attendants.

Bryan Laughlin
Founder and President
Concept Lighting, Design, and Special FX.

Bryanlaughlin@concepteffects.com

Born a true Chicagoan, Bryan has been heavily involved in the Halloween Industry for 15 years. Bryan studied Live Special FX at the Art Institute of Colorado and gained design experience while working in the Haunted House Industry and theater industry. Bryan has 12 years of experience designing atmospheres that revolve around a theme and are created to come to life. Bryan knows what people want when it comes to designing of an event. He has the experience and determination to create something from nothing and give the event life. His goal with Concept Effects is to give people the ability to make their dreams come to life through his design skills. By taking someone’s vision, he can create an atmosphere that will breathe life into any event and make them the Star.

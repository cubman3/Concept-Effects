DJ/Entertainment

Concept Effects pulls from our “tried and true” resources to provide professional DJ’s, MC’s, and entertainment for Weddings, graduations, corporate parties, bar/bat mitzvahs, birthday parties, and High school dances.

Your event day should be a unique experience and showcase of who you are as well as the message you want your guests to carry home. Our DJ’s and Entertainment will guide you through the key moments of your event and make it a night to remember.

Our professionals are the best in Chicago and allow you to relax and have the time of your life. We take our clients needs and vision into consideration when planning out the flow of your event, leaving our clients with their picture perfect event.

Catering

No event is complete with out a late night snack. Concept Effects gives our clients the ability to bring in that added touch. We offer a variety of catering options for your wedding, corporate/private event, graduation party, birthday/bat mitzvah, concert, theater performance, bridal shower, rehearsal dinner, and/or corporate meetings.

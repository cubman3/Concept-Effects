About Us

If You Can Dream It, We Can Do It.

Concept Effects is available to help you achieve your vision. Whether you are a lighting designer, corporate planner, production manager, event planner, wedding planner, concert promoter, private planner, or entertainment industry planner, Concept Effects is here to help you.

We will work hand in hand with you to provide you with top of the line lighting and special effects. Clients are encouraged to voice their vision and work with our designers to come up with their ideal event. Our goal is to provide in person consultation to help you get everything you need to make your event stand out and to work hand in hand with our clients and take whatever they are trying to showcase, whether it is a corporate event, or a wedding, and turn their event into a 5 star creation.

We always maintain our client’s budget needs no matter how large or small. We’re here to help you stand out amongst your peers. We will work with within your budget limits to provide you with the highest value products.

Whether you want to rent for one night or you are looking to add new products to your current inventory, we can help you.

Why Choose Concept?

We make it easy for you to host an event. Creativity is the key to a magnificent event and as a client we offer you just that, creative freedom. We will work with you to ensure the important aspects of your event are portrayed.

We understand that your visions come with a price; therefore, we work within your budget to help you create your vision. We provide an opportunity for your dreams to come true. Whether you are a corporate representative looking to showcase a product for an event, a bride who wants a dream wedding, or even a theater director planning a show or an event, we can assist you in any way. No budget is too small and no event is too big.

Concept Effects takes pride in providing you with flawless execution from the initial planning to the final invoice. The Process starts with an empty space, your vision, and your budget. Provide us with your thoughts, theme, and interests. Based on your vision and desire, we will work with you to create an unforgettable experience. Our designers will work with you to provide the best possible setting for your event. We want you to enjoy your moment and feel like a star.

We want to take the burden off your shoulders and let you enjoy your moment. As professionals, we aim for every event to be considered a top tier production. From set-up to tear down, we have a specialist on site to assure you that everything runs smoothly and so that you can enjoy your moment of stardom. Let us shoulder the stress for you.
